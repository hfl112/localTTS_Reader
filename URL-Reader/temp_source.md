To access library resources, log in using your UT Health San Antonio username and password. For more information about library access, see our **[Access to Library Resources](https://library.uthscsa.edu/access-to-library-resources/)** page.

[![UT Health San Antonio Login](https://d2jv02qf7xgjwx.cloudfront.net/accounts/128394/images/Login_ButtonSmall.png)](https://login.libproxy.uthscsa.edu/login?auth=shibboleth&url=r0$https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-026-10569-6)

For quicker, seamless access to library resources download **[LibKey Nomad](https://libguides.uthscsa.edu/libkey)**. LibKey Nomad is a browser extension that offers easy, one-click access to scholarly articles through the UT Health San Antonio Library.
